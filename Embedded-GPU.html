      Overview

      Silicon Motion provides cutting-edge embedded graphics solutions tailored for a wide array of applications, including industrial PCs, servers, high-resolution handheld devices, factory human-machine interfaces, point-of-sale terminals, medical equipment, and casino gaming machines. Our embedded GPU products are thoughtfully packaged within multi-chip modules (MCMs) featuring embedded video memory, offering an optimal balance between cost efficiency, power consumption reduction, and device longevity. The integration of fundamental graphics capabilities empowers our products with exceptional reliability and adaptability to cater to the diverse needs of various industries.

      The Silicon Motion Graphic Display SoCs have been meticulously crafted to cater to diverse application requirements.

      Features

      • Versatile Interface Support: Our embedded GPU solutions offer compatibility with multiple interfaces and operating systems, including USB, PCIe, Mini PCIe, and M.2 (PCIe).
      • To support a range of operating systems: Windows 7, 10, 11, and Linux.
      • Low Power Consumption: The SM768 model consumes less than 2.0 watts, ensuring consistent and energy-efficient performance.
      • Worldwide Technical Support Teams: We provide dedicated technical support teams globally to assist with customization and address your unique requirements.
      • Flexible OEM/ODM Services: allowing customers to tailor the product to their specific needs.

      Technologies

      Powering Your Vision with Multiple Interfaces and Dual-Channel Display

      The SM768 supports multiple interfaces and dual-channel displays, making it an excellent choice for various applications, from industrial automation to digital signage and more.

      The SM768 offers unparalleled versatility. Whether you need to integrate it as the primary display Graphic SoC with SMI Legacy/UEFI BIOS or use it as an extended display, the SM768 has you covered.
