                  Support our local communities

                  We take pride in the communities in which we operate, and we want them to grow and flourish with us. That is why we encourage good citizenship by actively supporting organizations that cultivate meaningful opportunities for civic engagement by our employees with members of the community, whether as volunteers or donors. Employees from our offices around the world regularly volunteer their time, resources and effort towards corporate-sponsored charity events and organize special days specifically dedicated to helping our community.

                  Charity Events

                  Please visit Silicon Motion charity for more details.

                  Taiwan

                  Our community actions have focused on education, healthcare and welfare for the disadvantaged and disabled, especially children. We support:

                  • Chang Show Jyu Social Welfare Foundation (張秀菊社會福利基金會)
                  • Syin-Lu Social Welfare Foundation (心路社會福利基金會)
                  • Taiwan Fund for Children and Families (台灣兒童暨家庭扶助基金會)
                  • Shih Guang Educational and Nursing Institution (世光教養院)
                  • Chung Yi Social Welfare Foundation (忠義社會福利事業基金會)
                  • Catholic Huakuang Social Welfare Foundation (華光社會福利基金會)
                  • Eden Social Welfare Foundation (伊甸社會福利基金會)
                  • Holy Rosary Training Center for Special Needs (玫瑰啟能訓練中心)
                  • SeeNeed Association (樂說多元專業服務發展協會)
